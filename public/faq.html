Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our platform, hiring process, and more.

For Candidates

How do I apply for a job?

Click on the "Apply" button on any job card. You'll be asked to provide your name, email, and resume. Then you'll record a 15‑second audio intro, optionally capture a photo, and select your preferred office location. Finally, you'll take a 30‑minute MCQ test. After submission, our team will review your application and get back to you within two weeks.

What file formats are accepted for resumes?

We accept PDF and DOCX files. Please ensure the file size is under 10 MB.

Is the camera mandatory for the application?

No, the photo is optional. However, the audio intro is mandatory as it helps us get to know you better.

What is the test format and duration?

The test consists of 20 multiple-choice questions covering C, C++, Computer Architecture, and OS basics. You'll have 15 minutes to complete it.

Can I retake the test if I'm not satisfied with my score?

Currently, you can only take the test once per application. However, you can apply for different job positions and take a new test for each.

How long does the hiring process take after applying?

Our team reviews applications within two weeks. You'll receive an email update about the status of your application.

Do I need to create an account before applying?

Yes, you need to sign up first. Your account stores your profile information and lets you track your applications.

What happens after I submit my test?

Your test score is automatically recorded. The hiring team reviews your resume, audio intro, and test score together. You can check your application status in "My Activity".

For Enterprises

How do I start hiring on QuantEdge?

Simply sign up for an enterprise account, post a job, and start receiving applications. You can also use our AI matching to find candidates.

Can I try before buying?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all enterprise plans. No credit card required.

How does pricing work for enterprises?

We offer flexible enterprise plans based on team size and features needed. Contact our sales team for a custom quote tailored to your hiring volume and requirements.

Can I integrate QuantEdge with our existing ATS?

Yes, QuantEdge provides REST APIs and webhooks to seamlessly connect with popular ATS platforms like Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, and more. Our integration team can assist with setup.

What kind of support do you offer?

Enterprise customers get 24/7 priority support via chat, email, and phone, along with a dedicated account manager and quarterly business reviews.

Can we customize the assessment tests?

Absolutely. Enterprise plans allow you to create custom tests with your own questions, branding, and time limits. You can also combine multiple skills into a single assessment.

Is there a mobile app for recruiters?

Yes, we offer a mobile app for iOS and Android that lets recruiters review candidates, send messages, and manage interviews on the go.

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

We comply with GDPR, SOC2, and ISO 27001. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we offer features like SSO, audit logs, and role-based access control.

Can we have multiple team members on one account?

Yes, enterprise accounts support multiple users with role‑based permissions (admin, recruiter, hiring manager, etc.) so your whole team can collaborate.

Do you offer training for our HR team?

We provide onboarding sessions, documentation, and ongoing training webinars to ensure your team gets the most out of QuantEdge.

What is the typical time‑to‑hire improvement?

Our customers typically see a 40% reduction in time‑to‑hire by automating screening, assessments, and interview scheduling.

Can we white‑label the platform?

Yes, enterprise plans include white‑labeling options so the platform reflects your company’s brand, including custom domain, logos, and email templates.
